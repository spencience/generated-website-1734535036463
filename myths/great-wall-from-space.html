This is one of the most persistent myths about human-made structures, but it's totally wrong! Here's why:

The Truth:

  • Astronauts have confirmed that the Great Wall is NOT visible to the naked eye from low Earth orbit (about 250 miles up)
  • Many other human structures, like airports and reservoirs, are actually more visible from space
  • The wall's color and material blend too well with the surrounding landscape
  • At most points, the wall is only about 5-8 meters wide - far too narrow to see from orbit

Origin of the Myth:

This misconception dates back to 1932 when Ripley's Believe It or Not! declared it "the mightiest work of man, the only one that would be visible to the human eye from the Moon." This claim was made decades before humans actually went to space!

Fun Fact: The Great Wall isn't even visible from the Moon! From there, no human-made structures can be seen with the naked eye.

What Astronauts Actually Say:

"It's certainly not visible from the Moon. It's barely visible from low Earth orbit." - Ed Lu, NASA Astronaut
