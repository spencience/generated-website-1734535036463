⚡ Lightning Never Strikes Twice

WRONG! Lightning absolutely strikes the same place multiple times!

The Shocking Truth

The Empire State Building gets struck by lightning approximately 25 times per year! In fact, tall buildings and structures are more likely to be struck multiple times due to their height and location.

Quick Facts:

  • The CN Tower in Toronto is struck around 75 times per year
  • Lightning rods are designed specifically to attract repeated strikes
  • One storm can produce multiple strikes in the exact same location
  • The phrase originated as a metaphor for unique events, not a scientific fact

Why Do People Believe This?

This myth likely persists because most people don't regularly observe lightning strikes, and the idea has been reinforced through popular culture and literature as a metaphor for unique events.

Lightning Safety Facts

Despite this myth being wrong, lightning safety is crucial. If you can hear thunder, you're close enough to be struck by lightning. Seek proper shelter immediately!
