About That's Totally Wrong!

Our Mission

We're on a relentless quest to separate fact from fiction in a world where misinformation spreads faster than truth. Founded in 2024, That's Totally Wrong! has become the go-to platform for debunking myths, misconceptions, and urban legends through rigorous fact-checking and community engagement.

Community-Driven

Our passionate community of truth-seekers helps identify and investigate common misconceptions.

Fact-Based Research

Every claim is thoroughly researched and verified by our team of expert fact-checkers.

Educational Impact

We've helped millions of people discover the truth behind common beliefs and misconceptions.

How It Works

1. Users submit commonly believed "facts" they suspect might be wrong

2. Our community votes on which submissions to investigate

3. Expert fact-checkers research the claims using credible sources

4. We publish detailed explanations with citations and evidence
